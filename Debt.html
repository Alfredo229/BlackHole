Fixing a debt in general

Introduction

The main problem with getting in debt, is you never saw that comming. One day you look at the money and you realize there's a problem. This webpage is intended to cover the basics of balancing a budget and gain control over our own money.

Structure

There's a few simple concepts to making a budget which should develop into sub concepts. From the top, there's income and expenses.

A lot of people will tell you sitting on a lot of money does nothing for you if you don't have an income. It washes away very fast. Print it, work it, steal it, make money. The possibilities are endless.

Expenses are somewhat more interesting. Categories for these depend on the lifestyle of an individual and the overall context of it's operations. Recursive bills is one of those and the counterpart would be daily operations. Daily operations are usually what sinks a budget as they are not easily managed due to their volatile nature.

Method

Making a budget itself doesn't solve anything. Solving problems is done in reality, not on paper. What a budget does is provide information for a tactical deployment of measures regarding finances. This information will let an individual decide on what immediate actions are to be had which will have an impact on his money.

The bills

Keep all the bills. The daily spending is what you will want to look at the first so make very sure you get as much information on it as possible.

The payments

These are slower and easier to document. But the consequences of moving those numbers can not be worth the gain. We're talking rent, transport, food... All of those.

The budget

I use Office Word. First page is going to be your month planning. Page breaks and new pages every month.

At the top you want your income and the detail of the calculation. If you have 2 jobs and tips, you write all of it and a total at the bottom.

Next you want the recurent bills. Heating, rent, internet, Spotify... Those monthly and predictable bills. List and total

Now for the method i am proposing. Income minus montlhy bills should be positive. If it is not well, it should be. Might want to look into a better income of maybe move to a different country. That sort of stuff. Hopefully that is not the case.

Now we have some money, yay! Time to spend it. There will be priorities like food or maybe gas for the car and other measures to support one's own lifestyle. That, is a daily thing. What we can do here, is plan it. What we'll do if count our money and alocate it weekly. Check the calendar, it should be 5 weeks, 4 maybe. So, you would divide all your money into say 5 weeks.

It's downhill from there, keep all the bills check against the amount of money you have left. Carry the balance from week to week. And don't spend money you don't have. If you have to do that, you have the knowledge to choose wisely. One thing though, you want to tweak the amount. Remove 20$ from it or any other amount you are comfortable with, weekly. This will alow you to well, save money and possibly pay for a debt.

You will make mistakes on the budget. This amount goes there, didn't calculate that... Don't fix it. Keep running with your current system in order to keep it coherent. Change your setup monthly.

Choices

The first thing that should come off is Amazon. It is easy to just click click and buy new pants. Then a new lamp. Then more things. If is very easy to do with the things you already have or otherwise find a way to deal with yourself. Other things are restaurent and probably grocery lists. It depends on what's on your daily spending lists.

Conclusion

It may not be possible to get out of debt, situations can be very complex. I calculated the American deficit to be that of 40,000$ per second. Making it 38,000$ per second would help, possible even but you know how it is. However, making a budget gives you power over your money and makes it do what you want. Don't be a slave.
